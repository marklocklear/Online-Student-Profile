Help Topics

View the list of classes that you are teaching

Look in the upper right hand corner of the page and hover over the ‘Classes’ menu.

A list of the classes you are currently teaching will be listed under the ‘Classes’ menu.

View the distribution of learning styles across the students in a class

Look in the upper right hand corner of the page and hover over the ‘Classes’ menu.

A list of the classes you are currently teaching will be listed under the ‘Classes’ menu.

Click on the desired class.

Information about the class will be displayed, with the distribution of learning styles at the top.

View a list of students in a class

Look in the upper right hand corner of the page and hover over the ‘Classes’ menu.

A list of the classes you are currently teaching will be listed under the ‘Classes’ menu.

Click on the desired class.

Information about the class will be displayed, with the class roster starting near the top.

The class roster includes the students’ names, learning styles, and personality types.

Contact one or more students in a class by email

Look in the upper right hand corner of the page and hover over the ‘Classes’ menu.

A list of the classes you are currently teaching will be listed under the ‘Classes’ menu.

Click on the desired class.

Information about the class will be displayed, including the class roster. There will be a checkbox next to each student’s name.

Specify which students you want to send the email to by checking the checkboxes next to their names.

Look at the bottom of the page and click the ‘Contact’ button.

A form will be displayed containing subject and message fields.

In the ‘Subject’ field, enter the subject of the email (or modify the pre-filled in subject).

In the ‘Message’ field, enter the message text of the email (or modify the pre-filled in message).

When you are ready to send the email, click the ‘Submit’ button.

The emails will be sent to the selected students.

Send an intervention notification on behalf of one or more students in a class

Look in the upper right hand corner of the page and hover over the ‘Classes’ menu.

You will see a list of the classes you are currently teaching under the ‘Classes’ menu.

Click on the desired class.

Information about the class will be displayed, including the class roster. There will be a checkbox next to each student’s name.

Specify which students you want to send an intervention for by checking the checkboxes next to their names.

Look at the bottom of the page and click the ‘Intervene’ button.

A form will be displayed containing a list of possible reasons for intervention, a campus drop-down, and subject and message fields.

Check the boxes next to the reasons that apply for this intervention notification.

Using the Campus drop-down, select the campus where this class is being held.

In the ‘Subject’ field, enter the subject of the intervention email notification (or modify the pre-filled in subject).

In the ‘Message’ field, enter the message text of the email (or modify the pre-filled in message).

When you are ready to send the intervention notification email, click the ‘Submit’ button.

The intervention notification email will be sent to the appropriate personnel.

View a student’s profile using search for student

Search for a student profile by the first name, last name, or email address of the student

Look in the upper right hand corner of the page for the ‘search for student’ search box.

In the ‘search for student’ search box, type in two or more characters to search for the start of a student’s first name, last name, or email address.

A list of matching students will be displayed below the ‘search for student’ search box.

Click on the desired student.

The profile of that student will be displayed.

View a student’s profile using a class roster

Look in the upper right hand corner of the page and hover over the ‘Classes’ menu.

A list of the classes you are currently teaching will be listed under the ‘Classes’ menu.

Click on the desired class.

Information about the class will be displayed, with the class roster starting near the top.

The class roster includes the students’ names, learning styles, and personality types.

Click on the name of the student whose profile you want to view.

The profile of that student will be displayed.

View a list of classes a student is currently taking

View the student’s profile.

To see a list of the classes a student is taking, look at the top of the profile under ‘Current Enrollments’

View information on a student’s learning style

View the student’s profile.

To see a summary graph of the student’s auditory, kinesthetic, and visual scores, look near the top of the profile under ‘Learning Style’

To see additional information about the student’s learning style, look to the right of ‘Learning Style’ and click the ‘view learning style details’ link

View information on surveys a student has taken

View the student’s profile.

To see a list of surveys a student has taken, look near the top of the profile, to the right of ‘Learning Style’, and under ‘Survey Results’

You will see a list of dates of when surveys were taken, along with links to view the details for each survey taken.

To view the details of a specific survey’s results, click the ‘view details’ link to the right of the desired survey’s date.

View information on a student’s personality type

View the student’s profile.

To see summary graphs of the student’s different personality type scores, look near the bottom of the profile under ‘Personality Type’

To see additional information about the student’s personality type, look to the right of ‘Personality Type’ and click the ‘view personality type details’ link

View a list of a student’s visits

View the student’s profile.

To see a list of the visits recorded for that student, look at the bottom of the profile under ‘Visits’

Log a student visit

View the student’s profile.

Look at the bottom of the profile to the right of ‘Visits’ and click the ‘log a visit’ link.

You will see a form containing the student’s name, and several form fields.

Fill in the form fields appropriately.

When you have completed filling out the visit information, click the Submit button to save the visit.

The visit will be saved and you will then see the visit in the table on the student's profile.

View a list of available reports

Look in the upper right hand corner of the page and hover over the ‘Reports’ menu.

A list of the types of reports you can run will be listed under the ‘Reports’ menu.

Generate a report of Personality Type assessment results

Look in the upper right hand corner of the page and hover over the ‘Reports’ menu.

A list of the types of reports you can run will be listed under the ‘Reports’ menu.

Click on ‘Personality Type’.

A form will be displayed containing fields for start and end dates.

Fill in the ‘Start Date’ and the ‘End Date’ to specify the range of dates during which Personality Type assessments were taken.

Click the ‘Submit’ button.

A report will be generated containing results of Personality Type assessments that were taken during the specified period.

From here you should be able to open or save the .xls report file.

Generate a report of Learning Style assessment results

Look in the upper right hand corner of the page and hover over the ‘Reports’ menu.

A list of the types of reports you can run will be listed under the ‘Reports’ menu.

Click on ‘Learning Style’.

A form will be displayed containing fields for start and end dates.

Fill in the ‘Start Date’ and the ‘End Date’ to specify the range of dates during which Learning Style assessments were taken.

Click the ‘Submit’ button.

A report will be generated containing results of Learning Style assessments that were taken during the specified period.

From here you should be able to open or save the .xls report file.
